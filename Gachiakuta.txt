🌍 The World & The Power SystemThe world is vertically divided between the Sphere (a floating utopia) and the Pit (a ground-level wasteland of trash).Vital Instruments (Jinki)In this world, objects have souls. Givers are individuals who can draw out the "Anima" from objects they cherish. When a Giver channels their spirit into a beloved object, it becomes a Vital Instrument (Jinki)—a weapon or tool with supernatural properties.The Watchman Series (Bannin Shirīzu)These are the six "Original" Vital Instruments, dating back to Rudo’s ancestor, Canis Surebrec.
